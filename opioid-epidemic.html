Visualizing Disease Vulnerability

NORC uses sophisticated digital tools to map the opioid epidemic

NORC is a pioneer in using geospatial mapping and other data visualization tools to help public health officials better understand and respond to a variety of risks. Working with the Appalachian Regional Commission, three units within NORC—The Walsh Center for Rural Health Analysis, our Health Media Collaboratory, and our Visualization Laboratory—are developing the infrastructure for an interactive geospatial data visualization tool that uses public data concerning the opioid crisis, analyses of social media posts, and other data sets to identify communities at particular risk of overdose deaths and other forms of mortality attributed to “diseases of despair.”
